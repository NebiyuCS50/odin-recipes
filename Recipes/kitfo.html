Kitfo

About Kitfo

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish, essentially a beef tartare, made with finely minced raw beef marinated in a spiced, clarified butter (niter kibbeh) and a chili-based spice blend (mitmita), often served with cheese (ayib) and greens (gomen).

Ingredient

  • Minced Raw Beef
  • Mitmita
  • Niter Kibbeh

Steps

  1. The minced beef is massaged with the mitmita and niter kibbeh
  2. The niter kibbeh is warmed to infuse the beef with its flavors without cooking it
