Doro Wote

About Doro-WOt

Doro wot, meaning "chicken stew" in Amharic, is a popular and iconic Ethiopian dish often considered the national cuisine. It's a spicy chicken stew, traditionally made with chicken, caramelized onions, berbere (a blend of spices), hard-boiled eggs, and served with injera, a spongy flatbread made from teff.

Ingredient

  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Berbere Spice
  • Niter Kibbeh
  • Onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • Tej

Steps

  1. The onions, garlic and ginger are caramelized
  2. Adding the chicken pieces and the spices
  3. The chicken is simmered for a long time for about an hour
  4. Then add the Niter Kibbeh
  5. Eggs are added near the end of the cooking time
